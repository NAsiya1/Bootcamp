About Me




Nombulelo Asiya

Greetings! My name is Nombulelo Asiya and I am delighted to introduce myself as a part-time tutor and the chairperson of a nonprofit organization called Hearts of Lavender. As an advocate for social justice and comprehensive sexuality education, I established this organization in 2022 to address the pressing issues of teenage pregnancy and other social challenges faced by children and youth. With a diverse vocational background in administration, customer service, magazine writing, and providing psychosocial support to students, I bring a wealth of knowledge and experience not only to Hearts of Lavender but any role. My primary goal is to create a society where all individuals are accepted and understood, while eradicating the negative impact of teenage pregnancy. Through Hearts of Lavender's workshops and seminars, we provide comprehensive sexuality education, and I am proud to say that I have obtained a facilitation qualification to effectively deliver these sessions. Furthermore, I have authored two cautionary books for the NPO, which aim to educate and empower children and youth to make responsible choices regarding their sexual health. I am passionate about making a positive impact, and I am determined to continue creating a brighter future for the next generation.
